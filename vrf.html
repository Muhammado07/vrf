Key Benefits

Energy efficiency

Zoning and Individual Control

Allows independent temperature control in different areas or rooms.

Design Flexibility

Offers flexibility in system design, with the ability to accommodate various building layouts, sizes, and occupancy patterns e.g., residential & commercial.

Quiet Operation

Designed to operate quietly, making them suitable for noise-sensitive environments such as hotels, libraries, and bedrooms as well as there is an option of the silent night mode that can be manually set, which lowers the noise level by about 8dBA at night.

Green Building Applications

R-410A Environmentally friendly refrigerant cools and heats more efficiently without destroying the ozone layer as well as the energy efficiency and precise control features to achieve sustainability.

Heat Recovery

Excess heat from zones in cooling mode can be recovered and redirected to areas requiring heating.
